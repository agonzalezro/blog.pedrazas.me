My Secret Valentine – I

By ipedrazas under AndroidIdea

Share

Last Wednesday I spent 3 hours with QME trying to make them understand that one thing is having an idea, a very different thing is creating a product from that idea.

I got an idea: to make an app for Saint Valentine called “My Secret Valentine”. A simple app that allows you to send a message (an image and a text) to your valentine.

First step: accelerated idea iteration, where you basically get that idea, you throw it into the washing machine of your brain and try to spin it as much as possible. The result? a much polished idea.

Second step: paper prototype. I’m a big fan of paper prototype. I always draw diagrams, relationships, anything… ideas on paper are great because if you cannot put your idea on a paper, you don’t have an idea, you’re day dreaming.

Paper prototypes for apps are the best. Some post-its and a pencil it’s enough to bring your app to live. Once I have the Post-It app done, I use a mobile outline where I try to do the equivalent of a storyboard of my app.

Once I have that I start coding… but that part will be on another post.

Stay Tuned!

The Challenge

By ipedrazas under AndroidIdea

Share

Yesterday I spent a few hours talking with the Queen Mary Entrepreneurs students. My main objective was to motivate those ones who had and idea to try and build a product. I’m not sure if I’ve succeeded or not but while I was talking I had the idea of teaching by giving example… so, here’s the challenge.

Today it’s 7th of February, in a week it will be 14th… Saint Valentine’s day, so the challenge is to build an Android app that allows you to send a Secret Saint Valentine’s message.

I have a week to design, implement, test and release this app. Can I do it? well, using Bob the Builder catchphrase: “Yes, we can!”

The idea is simple, a person uses the app to send an anonymous message to his secret Valentine. I don’t really know how the person will receive the message. It will be by email but I’m not sure of the format yet.

Anyway, the whole point is to write a bit about the experience so next time I meet with the QME people, I’ll have some real data to share with them.

Wish me luck!
